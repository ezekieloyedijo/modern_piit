Our Story

A Legacy of Innovation in
Tech Education

Prime Innovation Institute of Technology (PIIT) started its journey in 2009 as Prime Value County, driven by a mission to bridge the gap between education and technology. A year later, in 2010, we rebranded as Prime Innovation and earned accreditation from the National Board of Technical Education (NBTE). Initially established as a JAMB center, PIIT evolved rapidly, securing prestigious franchises with Aptech and Cisco in 2015. This strategic shift enabled us to specialize in high-demand IT disciplines such as software engineering, multimedia, and international certification training for TOEFL and IELTS.
With over a decade of experience, PIIT has built a strong reputation for producing job-ready graduates equipped with industry-relevant skills.
Our Programs: Shaping the Future of Tech Talent We offer a diverse range of programs tailored to equip students with in-demand skills:

Advanced Diploma in Software Engineering (ADSE) – Since 2015, we have empowered 470 graduates, with 188 currently enrolled.
Prime Diploma Certificate (PDC) – A foundation for aspiring IT professionals, with over 500 graduates.
Cisco Networking Academy (CISCO) – Training specialists in network security and infrastructure, graduating 145 students to date.
Industry Reach – PIIT has successfully trained and graduated over 5,000 students in various IT fields, many of whom have gone on to secure positions in top tech companies or start their own ventures.

Our Vision: Expanding IT Education for the Future In 2019, PIIT redefined its focus to offer specialized IT courses, discontinuing JAMB registrations to fully invest in developing Nigeria’s next generation of tech leaders. Our programs now include National Innovation Diploma (NID) pathways, enabling students to seamlessly continue into Higher National Diploma (HND) programs. With cutting-edge training, industry partnerships, and a commitment to excellence, PIIT continues to shape the future of IT education.
