What You Will Learn?

  • ROUTE

    Implementing Cisco IP Routing – Learn to plan, configure, and verify the implementation of complex enterprise LAN and WAN routing solutions, using arrange of routing protocols. ROUTE also covers configuration of secure routing solutions to support branch offices and mobile workers.

  • SWITCH

    Implementing Cisco IP Switched Networks – Learn to plan, configure, and verify the implementation of complex enterprise switching solutions, using Cisco Enterprise Campus Architecture. SWITCH also covers secure integration of VLANs, WLANs, voice, and video in to campus networks.

  • TROUBLESHOOT

    Trouble shooting and Maintaining Cisco IP Networks – Learn to
    (1) plan and perform regular maintenance on complex enterprise routed and switched networks, and
    (2) use technology-based practices and a systematic ITIL-compliant approach to perform network troubleshooting. Extensive labs provide hands-on learning and reinforce troubleshooting skills.
